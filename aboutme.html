About me

I am a graduate student and instructor of Physics at the National Institute of Physics (NIP), University of the Philippines Diliman (UPD). My pronouns are he/him.

I am taking up MS Physics since September 2020. I am currently a member of the recently established Data and Computation Group led by Dr. Reinabelle Reyes. You can read more about my research and teaching here. Before my MS stint, I was an undergraduate student at the same institute taking up BS Physics. I was a member of Dr. Jose Perico Esguerra's group under the Theoretical Physics Group. I also worked as an Undergraduate Student Assistant on the same research group. I graduated magna cum laude from BS Physics last July 2020.

Outside of graduate school, my interests include playing the guitar (and performing some gigs), listening to music (especially from the 70s-90s), and spending time with my friends over video games, gym, and anime. Disclaimer: these are just casual interests, I prefer not to involve myself too much in any of these.
