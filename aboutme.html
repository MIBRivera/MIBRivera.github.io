About me

I am a graduate student and instructor of Physics at the National Institute of Physics (NIP), University of the Philippines Diliman (UPD).

Professional

I am taking up MS Physics since First Semester, AY 2020-2021. I am currently a member of the recently established Data and Computation Group led by Dr. Reinabelle Reyes. You can read more about my research and teaching here.

Before my MS stint, I was an undergraduate student at the same institute taking up BS Physics. I was a member of Dr. Jose Perico Esguerra's group under the Theoretical Physics Group. I also worked as an Undergraduate Student Assistant on the same research group. I graduated magna cum laude last July 2020.
